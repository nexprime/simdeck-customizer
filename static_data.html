The Construction Process:
  1. Your custom layout is verified for electrical and mechanical compatability. If there are warnings of "Over budget" (too many inputs) or "Overlap" (modules colliding with each other) we will attempt to fix the problems where possible.
  2. The layout is then corrected for aesthetics - modules are alligned, centered, and squared-off as needed, while still keeping the overall layout the same.
  3. You will then receive a CAD rednering of the final deisgn and a PayPal invoice. If you're happy with the design, work will begin once the invoice is paid.
  4. Construction time varies based on the design complexity and part availibility - you will receive an estimate along with the final design rendering.
