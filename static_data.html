The Process:
  1. Your custom layout will be verified for electrical and mechanical compatibility. If there are warnings of "Over budget" (too many inputs) or "Overlap" (modules colliding with each other) we will attempt to fix the problems where possible.
  2. The layout is then corrected for aesthetics - modules are aligned, centered, and squared-off as needed, while still keeping the overall layout the same.
  3. You will then receive a CAD rendering of the final design and a PayPal invoice. If you're happy with the design, work will begin once the invoice is paid.
  4. Construction time varies based on the design complexity and part availability - you will receive an estimate along with the final design rendering.
  5. Once the assembly is complete and your custom SimDeck is shipped out, you will be updated with tracking information.
